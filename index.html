My Favourite Meal

Name of Meal: Mleqwa neDombolo,Samp, Butternut and Creamy spinach

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Hardbody chicken/Mleqwa
  • 2 teaspoons Salt
  • 4 cubes Chicken Stock
  • 3 cups Flour
  • 2 cups Lukewarm water
  • 2 tablespoons Yeast
  • 2 cups Samp
  • 2X50mg Butter
  • 4 teaspoons Aromat
  • qaurter cup Cremora
  • 1/2 Butternut
  • 2 tablespoons Sugar

Recipe

  1. To cook the chicken,Place the whole chicken in a large saucepan and add the water. Simmer over a medium heat for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  2. After an hour, add chicken stock, 1 teaspoon of aromat and half a teaspoon of salt. Add more water if necessary, but not too much.
  3. Reduce the heat and cook for another hour, or until the chicken is tender and falls off the bone.
  4. To cook the dombolo,In a large bowl, combine the yeast, sugar, and lukewarm water together and allow to sit for 8 – 10 minutes or until foamy.
  5. Add the flour and salt and mix until well combined. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead for 7 – 8 minutes or until smooth. Place into an oiled metal bowl and allow to rise for 1 hour.
  6. Once doubled in size, place the metal bowl into a simmering pot of water filled a quarter of the way up. Place the lid on and steam for 1 hour or until a skewer comes out clean.
  7. Remove the bowl from the simmering water and allow to cool slightly before removing the bread from the bowl. Slice and serve warm with butter and a hearty stew, soup, or curry.
  8. To cook the Creamy Samp,Bring the samp and water to the boil. Once boiling, lower the heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until soft. Drain off excess liquid if necessary.
  9. Add to the samp together with the Aromat and Cremora.
  10. Simmer over low heat for 5 minutes.
  11. Add the parsley and mix through. Serve warm.
  12. To cook the butternut, boil the butternut for 20 minutes. Then add butter and half a teaspoon of sugar.Serve warm.
